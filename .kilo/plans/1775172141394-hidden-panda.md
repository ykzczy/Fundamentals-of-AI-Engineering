## Design Guidelines for Custom Images

- **Architecture**: Clean box-and-arrow diagrams, no 3D effects
- **Color Palette**: Professional colors (blues, teals, muted greens, soft oranges) - avoid neon/bright colors
- **Text**: Minimal text, clear labels, sans-serif fonts
- **Layout**: Plenty of white space, left-to-right or top-to-bottom flow
- **Icons**: Simple geometric shapes instead of complex illustrations
